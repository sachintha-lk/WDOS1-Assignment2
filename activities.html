Activities

Hiking

Surrounded by the golden beaches is the interior of Sri Lanka offering you opportunities to hike through the misty mountain ranges and dense forests. You will be able to see unique plants and animals indigenous to the region. Hiking is one of the least expensive activities you can do, make sure you have all your gear and water to stay hydrated.

Click here to learn more

Snorkeling

Snorkeling is a very popular activity in the coastal areas. Near most popular beaches you will find snorkeling equipment for rent at a low price. Hikkaduwa is famous for it's magnificient coral reefs. If you choose to snorkel in Galle and Colombo you will get the opotunity to see some shipwrecks.

Click here to learn more

Surfing

Are you a beginner who want softer waves? Or are you an expert who needs a challenge? Or are you someone who wants to know how to surf? Sri Lanka's best surf spots got you covered. You can rent a surf board from a local shop near the beach. The prices vary from about 300LKR to 500LKR. You can also take surf lessons and it would cost you about 1300 an hour.
